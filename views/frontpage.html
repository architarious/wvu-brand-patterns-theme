Instructions:

  • Clone/download the WVU Brand Patterns theme from GitHub or Stash.
  • Create your own Stash repo in CleanSlate themes (link to instructions).
  • Copy the files you want from the WVU Brand Patterns theme into your new theme.
  • Edit your theme and compile your changes using your favorite Sass compiler.
  • Test your theme in Hammer (link to Hammer)
  • Push and sync your theme (link to instructions).
